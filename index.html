How to play:

Roll a die to find out how many spaces you can move up. After that, you will be presented with a historical trivia question related to the American Civil War. The difficulty of the question scales based off of how many spaces you may be able to move up. Get it correct, and you can move up that many spaces, get it wrong, and you move down half of however many spaces you rolled on the die (Odd numbers round floor, ie if you get a question wrong with a 3 die, you will move down 1 space insetad of 2). The first one to move a full lap around the board wins.
